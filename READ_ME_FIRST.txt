LEVEL UP, BITCH — PHONE + LAPTOP VERSION

WHAT THIS DOES
- Works on your Mac in Safari.
- Can be published as a website and added to your iPhone Home Screen.
- No Expo, VS Code, npm, Terminal, or coding required to use the app.

OPEN ON YOUR MAC
- Double-click index.html.

PUT IT ON YOUR IPHONE
1. Upload every file in this folder to a static website host.
2. Open the public link in Safari on your iPhone.
3. Tap Share.
4. Tap Add to Home Screen.

IMPORTANT ABOUT DATA
- This version saves entries in each browser.
- True automatic syncing between your iPhone and laptop requires connecting
  the app to your Supabase project using the project URL and anon public key.
